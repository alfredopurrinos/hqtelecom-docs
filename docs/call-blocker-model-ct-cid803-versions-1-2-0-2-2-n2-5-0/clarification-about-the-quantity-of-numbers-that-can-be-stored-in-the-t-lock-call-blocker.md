---
title: "Clarification about the quantity of numbers that can be stored in the T-Lock Call Blocker"
sidebar_position: 19
format: md
---

1,500 is the # of phone & fax numbers you can store in the
Blacklist memory.  

The 100 is the # of incoming calls that the unit stores in
the display memory. 

The 50 is the # of outbound calls the unit stores in the
display memory.

All the above numbers will be stored permanently in non-volatile memory.  This means that your blocked numbers till remained in memory even if you loose power.
