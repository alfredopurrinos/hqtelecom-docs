---
title: "How can I Unlock my Call Blocker?"
sidebar_position: 41
format: md
---

As explained in section 12.0 of the [manual](http://hqtelecom.com/TLock-Pro-CallBlocker-N2-UserManual.pdf).  The “LOCK” feature is turned "OFF" as a factory default setting.

If your T-Lock's
LCD displays "LOCK", then lift-off handset and input \*123456# (star key, then 1 2 3 4 5 6 then the pound key) to unlock (e.g. turn "OFF" the lock function), and
enable all T-Lock's functions.  Note: Make  sure you have a working handset connected to the PHONE socket on the Call Blocker.

To Lock it, you may lift-off handset and input \*000111# to turn "ON" the LOCK feature and LOCK all functions (LCD displays "LOCK").
