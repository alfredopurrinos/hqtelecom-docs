---
title: "Can I block calls that have no Caller ID info, and are shown as ‘unidentified’, or ‘unknown’, or ‘private’, or ‘Out of Area’, etc?"
sidebar_position: 18
format: md
---

Yes.
The T-LOCK has a new feature (refer to section 6 of the manual). This feature is turned “OFF” by default, however
if you turn it “ON”, it will block all incoming calls with no caller ID info.
