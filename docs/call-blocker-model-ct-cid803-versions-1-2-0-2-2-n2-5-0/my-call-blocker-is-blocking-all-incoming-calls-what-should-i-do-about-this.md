---
title: "My call blocker is blocking all incoming calls, what should I do about this?"
sidebar_position: 48
format: md
---

Here are some tips to help you resolve this issue.  We suggest you try these one at a time, starting with 1:  
  
1) Make sure feature SET 6 (see section 6 in the [manual](http://hqtelecom.com/TLock-Pro-CallBlocker-N2-UserManual.pdf)) is turned OFF.  
  
2) Try installing it in parallel using another phone jack or a splitter (refer to the manual for installation details)  
  
3) Review the BLACKLIST to make sure there are no 1 or 2 digits numbers stored (refer to section 8.2 in the [manual](http://hqtelecom.com/TLock-Pro-CallBlocker-N2-UserManual.pdf)). 

4) Reset the BLACKLIST memory by removing all the numbers (refer to section 10.2 in the [manual](http://hqtelecom.com/TLock-Pro-CallBlocker-N2-UserManual.pdf)).  

5) If none of the above works, please follow the instructions on our [warranty page](https://hqtelecom.com/warranty) to contact us and request a warranty repair or replacement.
