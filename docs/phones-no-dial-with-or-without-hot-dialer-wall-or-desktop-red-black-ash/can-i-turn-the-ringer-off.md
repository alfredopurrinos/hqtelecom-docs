sidebar_position: 1

Yes, if you wish you can turn the ringer OFF by cutting off the wire going to the ringer -- You will need to open it to access this wire.   
  
[Watch this video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kQMa43SESVY) on how to disable the ring bell.
