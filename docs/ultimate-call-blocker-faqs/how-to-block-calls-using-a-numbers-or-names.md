To manage your blocked and allowed numbers, **log in to your account** at:  
? <https://ultimatecallblocker.com/login>

Our user interface is designed to be **simple, intuitive, and easy to use**.  
You can block or allow numbers and names in the following ways:

---

#### **1) Add Entries Manually**

Use the **“Add Entry”** section to add numbers or names directly to your **Blacklist** or **Whitelist**.

- You can enter full or partial numbers/names.
- Choose the **Match Type**: *Exact Match*, *Contains*, *Starts With*, or *Ends With*.
- Select the **List Type** you want to add the entry to (Blacklist or Whitelist).

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/13169396937/original/K8c-FQ7g_Y4e7WCK2JXJYBlkkGp479ou5Q.png?1726893783)

**Important:**

- We **strongly recommend avoiding the manual method** for building your call lists. Instead, use the approach described in **Section 2** to create your **Blacklist** and **Whitelist** automatically.

  **Why?**  
  When you add numbers manually, you must know **exactly how the phone numbers are registered** on your phone line. Even small differences can prevent entries from working correctly.

  For example:

  - If you manually enter a local 10-digit number such as **305-512-1234**, but your phone system registers calls as **1-305-512-1234**, the block will **not** take effect.
  - The same applies to names. If you want to block calls with “FLORIDA” in the name field, but your incoming calls display only “FL” or “FLO,” the entry will **not match** and will fail to block the call.
- By using the method in Section 2, your UBlocker automatically recognizes how numbers and names are logged, helping you avoid these common errors.

#### **2) Move Calls from the New List**

You can also block or allow numbers directly from your **New List**:

1. Open the **New List** section.
2. Click the buttons labeled **“Move to Whitelist”** or **“Move to Blacklist”** under each number.

**Important:**

- We highly recommend you use this method to build your Blacklist and Whitelist instead of the doing it manually.
- The ability to move calls from your New List becomes available as incoming calls start populating your New List. So, when you first setup your UBlocker you will not be able to do this.  Just wait a few days so that new calls come in, and then you will be able to start building your Blacklist and Whitelist.

---

#### **3) Use the Global Blacklist Search**

Search for a number in the **Global Blacklist Search** section to see if it’s already listed in our global database.

- If the number **appears in the Global Blacklist**, it is **automatically blocked** — you do not need to add it again.
- If you want to **receive calls** from a number that appears on the Global Blacklist, add it to your **Whitelist** to override the global filter.
