The Global Blacklist enhances your UBlocker's functionality by automatically blocking over 500,000 numbers (and growing at a rate of thousands daily) linked to scams and spams in the US and abroad. 

Our team is constantly adding new numbers linked to new unsolicited calls including scams, robocalls, political calls, etc.  

Our community of thousands of UBlocker owners also benefit from each other's efforts.  Our server is programmed to automatically add numbers to the Global BlackList when 2 or more users add a specific number.  So, it is not just you working to block unwanted calls -- Now you have a whole community doing this together!
