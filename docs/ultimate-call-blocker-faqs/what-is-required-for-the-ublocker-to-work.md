---
title: "What is required for the UBlocker to work?"
sidebar_position: 6
format: md
---

- A landline phone line (can be POTs, cooper-based, Cable-based, or VoIP).  
- Caller ID service active (e.g. the ability to read name & phone numbers on your existing handsets). 

- An Internet connection (UBlocker version LAN requires an ethernet connection on your router or switch, UBlocker WL can connect via WIFI).  
- You also need a regular phone or handset with CallerID display  
- You also need a PC, smartphone or tablet to manage your device online.
