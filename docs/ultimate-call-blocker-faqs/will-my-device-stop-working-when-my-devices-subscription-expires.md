---
title: "Will my device stop working when my device's subscription expires?"
sidebar_position: 12
format: md
---

Your UBlocker may continue to block calls, however you will not be able to manage your lists online unless you pay a yearly maintenance fee.  This fee allows us to cover the recurring costs associated with maintaining the website up and running, and also maintaining the Global Blacklist.   

Your device(s) will be restored and all data and lists will show again upon completing your payment. Please allow 1-2 business hours after completing your payment to get your account re-activated.
