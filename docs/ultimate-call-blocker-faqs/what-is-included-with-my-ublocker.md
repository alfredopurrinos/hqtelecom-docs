title: "What is included with my UBlocker?"
sidebar_position: 2
format: md
---

Your UBlocker package includes the following:

**(1) UBlocker Device**  
– The main unit, powered by a Raspberry Pi mini computer.

**(1) Power Adapter**  
– Provides power to the UBlocker.  
– Includes a micro USB plug (connects to the UBlocker) and a standard U.S. plug (for any 120V outlet).

**(1) Phone Adapter (USB Modem)**  
– Allows the UBlocker to connect to your landline phone jack or modem port.  
– Plugs into any of the four USB ports on the UBlocker.  
– Includes two RJ11 sockets:  
 • **LINE:** Connects to your wall jack or modem port.  
 • **PHONE:** Connects to your phone or phone system.  
– An RJ11 patch cord is included.

****(1) Ethernet Patch Cable with Two RJ45 Plugs**** – Used to connect the UBlocker directly to your router.  
 – A wired connection is highly recommended for the most reliable Internet performance.

**(1) Documentation (Installation Instructions & User Manual)**  
– Step-by-step setup guide for your UBlocker device.  
– Contains your UBlocker’s **unique ID and Key** (case-sensitive).  
– These codes are required to link your device to your online account.
