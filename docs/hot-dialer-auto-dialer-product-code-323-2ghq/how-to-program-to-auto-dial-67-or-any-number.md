---
title: "How to program to auto dial *67 (or * + any number)?"
sidebar_position: 3
format: md
---

Here are the steps:  
  
\* \* \*   (login)  
\* \* 9 \* \*  (reset)  
\* 9 \* 1 \* \* 200 \* # # 67 \* \*  (program number to auto dial)  
\* \* 241 \*  (changes # by \*)  
\* \* 235 \*  (enable off-hook dialing)  
  
If you need any more help, please contact us.
