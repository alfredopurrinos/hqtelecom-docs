title: "Can I block 10-digit numbers?"
sidebar_position: 6
format: md
---

Yes!  We added a new feature (as of 6-12-2021) to allow blocking of up to 3 10-digit numbers on our Programmable Outbound Call Blocker (Product 3232GHQ)  
Product details:  <https://www.hqtelecom.com/callblockers/outgoingcallblockers/blocker>  
  
For example, below are the programming steps to block 305-512-1234 or 1-305-512-1234

\*\*\*  (hear a beep)

\*\*9\*\*  (hear a beep)

\*\*5\*\*5121234\*\*  (hear a beep)

\*\*59\*305\*1305\*\* (hear a beep)  
  
Tip: If at any time you hear 2 beeps, you need to re-enter that commend until you hear 1 beep.  
  
Similarly, you can block up to 3 10-digit numbers as follows:  
  
Blocking numbers: 305-512-1111, 786-512-2222, and 954-512-3333 (including 1+)

\*\*\*  (hear a beep)

\*\*9\*\*  (hear a beep)

\*\*5\*\*5121111\*5122222\*5123333\*\*  (hear a beep)

\*\*59\*305\*1305\*786\*1786\*954\*1954\*\* (hear a beep)
